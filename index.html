Nduma Nyama

Peel arrowroot, cut into pieces, wash, and let them dry. Peel potatoes, cut into small pieces.
2. Add cooking oil in a pot. Fry onions under medium heat until golden brown, then add ginger and garlic, fry for 2 minutes. Add beef and cook for about 10 minutes.
3. Add arrow roots, potatoes, and cover for 2 minutes as you stir. Add blended tomatoes, cooking cream, and a pinch of salt to taste.
